**Content structure (~1500 words, per D-11):**

1. **H1:** "How We Rank Products" (text-2xl font-semibold)
2. **Last updated:** "Last updated: March 26, 2026" (text-sm text-muted-foreground, below H1)
3. **Intro paragraph** (2-3 sentences): Per UI-SPEC -- "Our product rankings are based on consumer benefit, not advertising revenue. Here is exactly how we evaluate and compare financial products."

4. **H2: "Our Independence Promise"** (per D-12)
   Body: Per UI-SPEC Copywriting Contract -- "Commission rates do not influence our rankings. We rank products based on their value to you: total cost, rewards value, eligibility breadth, and customer experience. If a product pays us more but serves you worse, it ranks lower."
   Expand to ~150 words covering: how we make money (affiliate links, clearly disclosed), why commission does not affect ranking, what happens if a top product has no affiliate link (we still list it).

5. **H2: "How We Rank Credit Cards"**
   Criteria list (~200 words):
   - Total cost to the cardholder (APR weight, annual fee impact)
   - Rewards value relative to spending patterns
   - Signup bonus value and attainability
   - Credit score accessibility
   - Consumer protections and benefits
   Explain that lower total cost and higher net rewards value rank higher.

6. **H2: "How We Rank Personal Loans"**
   Criteria list (~200 words):
   - APR range (lower is better for equivalent terms)
   - Fee transparency (origination fees, prepayment penalties)
   - Loan amount flexibility
   - Term options
   - Eligibility breadth (wider credit score acceptance ranks higher)

7. **H2: "How We Rank Savings Accounts & CDs"**
   Criteria list (~200 words):
   - APY (higher is better)
   - Minimum deposit requirements (lower barrier ranks higher)
   - Fee structure (monthly maintenance, early withdrawal penalties)
   - FDIC insurance confirmation
   - Access flexibility (for savings) or term options (for CDs)

8. **H2: "How We Rank Insurance"**
   Criteria list (~200 words):
   - Coverage value per premium dollar
   - Deductible options and flexibility
   - Claims satisfaction and customer ratings
   - Discount availability
   - Coverage completeness for the tier

9. **H2: "Where Our Data Comes From"**
   Body (~150 words): We collect product data from official issuer websites, public rate disclosures, and regulatory databases including the CFPB. We verify rates, terms, and eligibility directly with product providers where possible. Include: "Our ranking approach follows principles outlined by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau for fair product comparisons."

10. **H2: "How Often We Update"**
    Body: Per UI-SPEC -- "We review and update product data monthly. Rates and terms change frequently. The date shown in each comparison table reflects the last verification."
    Expand to ~100 words covering: monthly review cycle, what triggers an off-cycle update (product discontinuation, rate change), how users can report stale data.

11. **Cross-reference link:** Per UI-SPEC -- "Read our full [editorial standards](/editorial-standards) for more on how we create content." Use a Link component.

12. **Back link:** Link to "/compare" -- "Back to product comparisons."
